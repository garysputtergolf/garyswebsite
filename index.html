Our Story

A Hidden Gem in Northern PA

Our History

Nestled in the quiet hills since 1961, Gary's has been a family-run business for over 60 years. A go-to destination in Potter County.

Our Location

Surrounded by 390 square miles of state forests. Our 100+ acre property features beautiful pines, a pond, and a bustling creek.

Amenities

From zip-lining and gem mining to our famous 27-hole miniature golf course. Fun for the whole family.

Camping & Cabins

Stay with us! We offer cabins, RV spots, and primitive tent camping. Experience nature with comfort.
