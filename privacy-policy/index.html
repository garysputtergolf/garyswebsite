Privacy Policy

How we respect and safeguard your privacy at Gary's Family Resort.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Gary's Family Resort, we believe in complete privacy. Our website is a static information portal designed solely to share resort details, menu prices, and booking resources. We do not collect, store, track, or share any of your personal data when you visit this website.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information (such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number) as you browse our pages. There are no registration forms, contact portals, or subscription fields on this site that capture your private information.

No Cookies or Telemetric Tracking

We value a clean, non-intrusive browsing experience. This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts to monitor, profile, or follow your behavior online.

Hosting & Server Logs

This website is hosted securely using GitHub Pages. When you visit our website, GitHub's servers may automatically log basic technical information for standard operational and security purposes. This information includes your IP address, browser type, referring website, and the date/time of your visit. These logs are maintained by GitHub to prevent abuse, mitigate DDoS attacks, and ensure the stability of the hosting platform. We do not have access to these raw server logs, nor do we store or use this data ourselves.

Reservations & Third-Party Services

Reservations at Gary's Family Resort are processed externally through a secure third-party booking platform, CampLife. When you click "Book Now" or "Reserve Your Stay", you are redirected to the secure CampLife platform. Any personal or billing details you provide during the booking process are processed and secured directly by CampLife under their own privacy practices. We do not process, store, or receive your credit card or financial details.

We encourage you to review CampLife's Privacy Policy to understand their secure data operations.

Changes to This Policy

Because we do not collect any user data, the core nature of this privacy policy is highly stable. Should we ever alter this policy for operational or legal reasons, we will post the updated terms on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, or if you choose to contact us directly via email or telephone, we will only use your details to respond to your specific request. We never sell, share, or store your contact information for marketing or advertising purposes.
